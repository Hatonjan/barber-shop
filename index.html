Get to know our crew

Highly skilled professionals committed to your best look

Malik “The Vibe” Johnson

Fade Master & Culture Curator

Malik’s chair is where style meets storytelling. He’s got a sixth sense for blending classic cuts with modern edge—whether it’s a skin fade, twist-up, or a clean taper. Clients come for the cut, but stay for the energy. When he’s not shaping hair, he’s spinning neo-soul playlists and mentoring local youth through style workshops.

Ethan “The Architect” Reynolds

Senior Stylist & Precision Specialist

With over 12 years in the game, Ethan brings a calm, methodical energy to the shop. Known for his razor-sharp fades and geometric beard work, he’s the guy clients trust when they want something bold but balanced. Outside the shop, Ethan’s a vinyl collector and mid-century design nerd—he sees every haircut as a blueprint for confidence.

Diego “The Detail” Morales

Texture Expert & Creative Lead

Diego’s artistry lives in the details—from crisp line-ups to layered curls that move with intention. He’s fluent in texture, and his cuts reflect a deep respect for individuality. Raised in a family of barbers, Diego blends tradition with innovation. He’s also the shop’s unofficial photographer, capturing fresh cuts and candid moments for the crew’s socials
